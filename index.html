Circus In A Flash™

We'll make your event awesome!

We bring the circus to you, with circus-style entertainment for all types of parties, events, workshops, promotions and celebrations throughout New Zealand – stilt-walking, crowd interaction, unicycling, juggling, acrobatics and more, with highly skilled performers in incredible costumes.

As of December 2021 we are concentrating our services in Tauranga, the Bay of Plenty and Hamilton. We will no longer be offering kid's parties in other regions but will still be available for events and holiday programmes throughout the North Island. We do not service the South Island unless specific events or where travel fees are paid. We look forward to moving forward to continue providing great quality entertainment in this new Covid landscape!
